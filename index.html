About Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson (1958–2009) is celebrated as the “King of Pop.” He helped redefine performance and music videos with releases like Thriller, Bad, and Dangerous, while popularizing moves such as the moonwalk and the lean.

This game is a tribute to his iconic stagecraft, fashion, and dance vocabulary. Explore his legacy, music, and humanitarian work to learn more about the artist who shaped modern pop culture.
